<h1>☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)</h1>

<h2>📌 Project Overview</h2>

This project presents an interactive Coffee Shop Sales Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI. The dashboard provides actionable insights into sales performance, product trends, store comparisons, and time-based purchasing behavior. The objective of this project is to simulate a real-world retail analytics scenario and demonstrate strong capabilities in: 
* Data modeling
* DAX calculations
* Business KPI development
* Visual storytelling
* Dashboard design best practices

<h2>🧾 Dataset Information</h2>

The dataset contains transactional sales data for a coffee shop chain, including:
* Order Date
* Product Category & Product Type
* Store Location
* Quantity Sold
* Sales Amount
* Order Details

<h2>🎯 Business Objectives</h2>

* Monitor overall sales performance
* Compare Month-over-Month growth
* Identify top-performing products and categories
* Analyze weekday vs weekend sales behavior
* Evaluate store-level performance
* Understand hourly sales distribution patterns

* Total Sales
* Total Orders
* Total Quantity
* Month-over-Month Growth %
* Previous Month Sales (using DATE functions)
* Average Daily Sales
* Ranking for Top 10 Products
* Dynamic filtering with slicers

<h2>🚀 Key Insights</h2>

* Coffee category contributes the highest revenue share.
* Weekday sales significantly outperform weekend sales.
* Morning hours (8 AM – 10 AM) show peak customer activity.
* All 3 stores show consistent Month-over-Month growth.
* A small group of products drives a large portion of revenue (Pareto effect).
